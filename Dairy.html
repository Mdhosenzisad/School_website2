SSC Short Syllabus 

During the countrywide shutdown of schools, SSC short syllabus 2022 has become one of the most discussed topics among the students, teachers, and guardians. Everyone is anxiously waiting for this situation to come to an end so that young students can go back to their school premises.

Taking public exams like SSC is also essential but the ministry of education is worried that students might not be able to complete the whole syllabus as the academic year is already shortened. So, the only way is to publish or the sake of the betterment of students. Let’s get to know more about it.

SSC Short Syllabus 2022

 

The students who are going to take the SSC exam in the next year now have very little time to cover their syllabus. It will be of immense pressure on them if they had to complete this syllabus within this short period.

Check also: 


So, the government has decided to shorten the syllabus and publish the SSC short syllabus for the students. This short syllabus will be published the next month. According to Dr. Dipu Moni, Minister of the ministry of education, the govt has made the SSC short syllabus 2022 so that students can complete it in only 150 days.
